Smart UI desk

By Nachiket Dighe

Based on interview feedback collected, Features include:

  • Different app options for different users
  • Ability to add/delete to-do notes
  • Slider to adjust height of smart desk
  • Notifications panel that imports data from apps such as Canvas or calender
  • Simple UI that is not too complex
  • Ability to connect your phone and sync data
  • In-built charger with icons showing charging percentage
